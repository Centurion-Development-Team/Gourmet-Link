Guacamole
Jerald kong
#Appetizers #Vegetarian #Desserts #Mexican

Ingredients

3 ripe avocados
2 Roma tomatoes
1 jalapeño pepper
¼-½ cup finely diced red onion
2-3 TBSP chopped fresh cilantro
1 small lime
Guacamole seasoning (recipe below)

Homemade Guacamole Seasoning:
1 tsp garlic powder
½ tsp onion powder
½ tsp dried cilantro
¼ tsp ground cumin
¼ tsp ground cayenne pepper
¼-½ tsp salt

Steps

Step 1: Mix seasoning spices in a small bowl.
Step 2: Mash avocados in a bowl, then add diced tomatoes, jalapeño, red onion, cilantro, lime juice, and seasoning.
Step 3: Adjust lime and seasoning to taste. Serve with tortilla chips.
