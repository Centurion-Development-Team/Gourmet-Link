Shepherd's Pie
Elise Bauer
#Comfort Food #Casserole #Desserts #British Cuisine

Ingredients

1 teaspoon salt
3 large potatoes
8 tablespoons butter
1 medium onion
1-2 cups mixed vegetables
1 1/2 pounds ground beef
1/2 cup beef broth
1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
Pepper and seasonings

Steps

Step 1: Boil peeled potatoes until tender, then mash with butter.
Step 2: Sauté onions and vegetables, then add ground beef, Worcestershire sauce, and broth. Simmer.
Step 3: Layer meat mixture and mashed potatoes in a casserole dish. Bake at 400°F for 30 minutes, broiling if needed for extra browning.
