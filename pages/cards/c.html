Ingredients

Cremini or white button mushrooms
Extra-virgin olive oil (Spanish Hojiblanca preferred)
Salt and black pepper
Baby spinach
Roasted red peppers (store-bought or homemade)
Yellow onions
Minced garlic cloves
Italian seasoning (homemade or store-bought)
Whole milk ricotta cheese
Parmesan cheese
Minced parsley (for garnish)

Steps

Step 1: Preheat oven to 375°F.
Step 2: Wash and de-stem mushrooms, reserving stems. Season caps with olive oil, salt, and pepper, then place hollow side up on a baking sheet.
Step 3: In a skillet, heat olive oil and cook chopped mushroom stems, roasted red pepper, onion, garlic, and seasoning until softened. Add spinach and cook until wilted.
Step 4: Transfer mixture to a bowl, stir in ricotta.
Step 5: Stuff mushroom caps with filling, top with parmesan, and bake for 20 minutes. Broil for 2-3 minutes to crisp cheese. Garnish with parsley and serve.
