Cinnamon Rolls
Lord Maximus
Prep time: 30 min | Diffeculty: Medium | 4 Servings | #Dessert

Details

These easy cinnamon rolls are perfect for yeast beginners because they only require 1 rise. You have a few options for toppings. The recipe below includes a simple cream cheese icing, but we also love these with the coffee icing or vanilla icing that’s included in the recipe Notes below.

Ingredients

All Purpose Flour
Granulated Sugar
Instant Yeast
Butter (unsalted or salted)
Milk (whole)
Eggs
Cinnamon
Brown Sugar
Pure Vanilla Extract
Cream Cheese
Powdered Sugar

Steps

Step 1: Make and knead dough until it passes the window pane test. Let rise for 1 hour.
Step 2: Roll out dough, spread filling, and roll up. Slice into 12 pieces and place in a baking pan. Let rise for another hour.
Step 3: Bake for 30 minutes until golden brown. Top with cream cheese icing and enjoy!
