Mini Quiche
Sally
Prep time: 1hr | Diffeculty: Hard | 4 Servings | #Lunch

Details

Easier and quicker than the full size version, my mini quiche uses a buttery flaky pie crust and a simple 4-5 ingredient egg filling. You can add a variety of flavors and add-ins like ham, bacon, spinach, Swiss cheese, scallions, mushrooms, peppers, and more. Bake these mini quiches in a mini muffin pan and watch them virtually disappear. I absolutely love quiche and this tiny version is even better!

Ingredients

Pie Crust
Heavy Cream and Milk
Eggs
Salt and Pepper
Optional Add-ins: Ham, Bacon, Spinach, Swiss Cheese, Scallions, Mushrooms, Peppers

Steps

Step 1: Prepare pie dough and chill for 2 hours.
Step 2: Make egg filling and chill.
Step 3: Roll out dough, cut into small circles, and fit into mini muffin pan.
Step 4: Fill each crust with egg filling and top with add-ins.
Step 5: Bake until crust is browned.
