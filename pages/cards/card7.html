Mini Quiche
Sally
#Bite-Sized Snacks #Brunch #Desserts #Party Food

Ingredients

Pie Crust
Heavy Cream and Milk
Eggs
Salt and Pepper
Optional Add-ins: Ham, Bacon, Spinach, Swiss Cheese, Scallions, Mushrooms, Peppers

Steps

Step 1: Prepare pie dough and chill for 2 hours.
Step 2: Make egg filling and chill.
Step 3: Roll out dough, cut into small circles, and fit into mini muffin pan.
Step 4: Fill each crust with egg filling and top with add-ins.
Step 5: Bake until crust is browned.
