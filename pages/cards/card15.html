Basil Pesto Sauce
Marcie
#Sauces #Italian Cuisine #Meal Prep

Ingredients

4 cups basil leaves
Olive oil
Garlic
Lemon juice
Parmesan cheese
Pine nuts

Steps

Step 1: Toast pine nuts over medium-low heat until golden and fragrant.
Step 2: Blend basil, garlic, lemon juice, Parmesan, pine nuts, and salt in a food processor until coarsely chopped.
Step 3: With the processor running, add olive oil until the desired consistency is reached.
