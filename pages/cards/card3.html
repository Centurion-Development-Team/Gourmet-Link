Grilled Pork Chops
Erin Clarke
#Grilling #Main Course

Ingredients

Thick-cut, bone-in pork chops
Pork rub (salt, garlic, lemon zest, rosemary)
Fresh herbs (optional, for garnish)

Steps

Step 1: Pat pork chops dry, rub with the seasoning mix, and let sit for 1 hour or overnight.
Step 2: Grill pork chops over medium-high heat for 5 minutes per side, until they reach 135°F.
Step 3: Let rest for 10 minutes before serving.
