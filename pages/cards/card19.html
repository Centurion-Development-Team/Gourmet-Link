Frozen Yogurt
Lord Maximus
Prep time: 5 min | Diffeculty: Easy | 2 Servings | #Dessert

Details

This frozen yogurt recipe takes only 5 minutes of work, and you do not need an ice cream maker! Be sure to check out my tips for how to make frozen yogurt that is creamy and delicious.

Ingredients

2 cups plain yogurt
2 cups plain Greek yogurt
¾ cup sugar
2 tablespoons honey
Fruit toppings (optional)

Steps

Step 1: Whisk yogurts, sugar, and honey until sugar dissolves.
Step 2: Freeze according to your ice cream maker's instructions.
Step 3: Serve immediately with toppings or freeze until needed, softening before serving if necessary.
