Hummus
Maria Lichty
#Vegetarian #Middle Eastern

Ingredients

Canned chickpeas
Baking soda
Tahini
Fresh lemon juice
Olive oil
Garlic
Salt
Ice water

Steps

Step 1: Cook chickpeas with baking soda until mushy, then rinse.
Step 2: Blend chickpeas, tahini, lemon juice, garlic, and salt until smooth.
Step 3: Gradually add ice water until creamy. Adjust seasoning, then serve with olive oil and toppings.
