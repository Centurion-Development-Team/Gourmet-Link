Bruschettae
Juliakim
Prep time: 50min | Diffeculty: Medium | 10 Servings | #Dinner

Details

Bruschetta was made for warm weather snacking. Easy to throw together and made up of simple, classic ingredients, it's the perfect dish to serve when you're craving something classic and refreshing. While you can make it all year round, the best time to enjoy this appetizer is summer, when fresh tomatoes are at their peak.

Ingredients

6-7 ripe tomatoes
2 cloves garlic, minced
1 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
1 tsp balsamic vinegar
6-8 fresh basil leaves
3/4 tsp sea salt
1/2 tsp freshly ground black pepper
1 baguette
1/4 cup olive oil

Steps

Step 1: Blanch tomatoes, peel skins, and remove seeds.
Step 2: Chop tomatoes and mix with garlic, olive oil, balsamic vinegar, basil, salt, and pepper.
Step 3: Toast baguette slices with olive oil in a 450°F oven, then top with tomato mixture and serve.
