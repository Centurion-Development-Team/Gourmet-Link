Bruschettae
Elise Bauer
#Appetizers #Snacks #Italian #Party Food

Ingredients

6-7 ripe tomatoes
2 cloves garlic, minced
1 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
1 tsp balsamic vinegar
6-8 fresh basil leaves
3/4 tsp sea salt
1/2 tsp freshly ground black pepper
1 baguette
1/4 cup olive oil

Steps

Step 1: Blanch tomatoes, peel skins, and remove seeds.
Step 2: Chop tomatoes and mix with garlic, olive oil, balsamic vinegar, basil, salt, and pepper.
Step 3: Toast baguette slices with olive oil in a 450°F oven, then top with tomato mixture and serve.
