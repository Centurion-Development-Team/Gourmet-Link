Hot Spinach Artichoke Dip
Melissa Stadler
#Party Food #Cheesy Dips

Ingredients

Frozen spinach (thawed and drained)
Artichoke hearts (drained and chopped)
Cream cheese (softened)
Mayonnaise (full-fat)
Sour cream (full-fat)
Parmesan cheese
Mozzarella, white cheddar, or Monterey Jack cheese (grated)
Garlic (minced)
Salt and pepper
Red pepper flakes (optional)

Steps

Step 1: Thoroughly drain spinach and artichoke hearts. Squeeze spinach and blot with paper towels.
Step 2: Mix cream cheese, mayonnaise, sour cream, Parmesan, and grated cheese with minced garlic. Season with salt, pepper, and red pepper flakes.
Step 3: Bake until golden and bubbly. Serve warm with tortilla chips, crackers, or bread.
