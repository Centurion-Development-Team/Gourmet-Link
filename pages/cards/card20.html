Cookie Ice Cream Sandwiche
Lord Maximus
Prep time: 1 hr | Diffeculty: Medium | 3 Servings | #Dessert

Details

There’s nothing quite like a chocolate chip cookie, except for two chocolate chip cookies sandwiched together with ice cream. These cookie ice cream sandwiches use a quick no-chill cookie dough recipe that produces soft cookies perfect for freezing. I strongly recommend using mini chocolate chips, and be sure to freeze the assembled sandwiches for at least 3 hours prior to serving.

Ingredients

2 and 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
1 and 1/2 teaspoons cornstarch
1 teaspoon baking soda
1/2 teaspoon salt
3/4 cup unsalted butter, softened
3/4 cup packed brown sugar
1/2 cup granulated sugar
1 large egg + 1 egg yolk
2 teaspoons pure vanilla extract
1 and 1/4 cups mini semi-sweet chocolate chips
3 cups vanilla ice cream
1 cup mini chocolate chips, sprinkles, or finely chopped nuts

Steps

Step 1: Preheat oven to 350°F and line baking sheets with parchment. Mix dry ingredients in a bowl.
Step 2: Cream butter and sugars, then add egg, yolk, and vanilla. Combine with dry ingredients and chocolate chips.
Step 3: Scoop dough onto baking sheets, bake for 12–13 minutes. Cool, then assemble with ice cream and roll in toppings. Freeze until firm.
