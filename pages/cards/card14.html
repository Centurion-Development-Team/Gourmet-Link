Tzatziki
Anna Smith
Prep time: 45 min | Diffeculty: Medium | 2 Servings | #Dips

Details

This fresh tzatziki recipe will liven up any summer meal! Rich, creamy & full of fresh herbs, it's delicious on pita, grilled veggies, sandwiches, and more!

Ingredients

1½ cups plain Greek yogurt
1 medium cucumber (coarsely grated and squeezed dry)
2 garlic cloves (minced)
2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
¾ teaspoon salt
¼ teaspoon black pepper
2 tablespoons fresh mint (chopped)

Steps

Step 1: Mix all ingredients in a bowl until combined.
Step 2: Taste and adjust seasoning, adding a pinch of sugar if needed.
Step 3: Chill until ready to serve, then drizzle with olive oil and sprinkle with mint.
