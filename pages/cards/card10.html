Chicken Kabob
Eren clarke
#Grilled Dishes #Mediterranean #Healthy

Ingredients

Boneless skinless chicken breasts (or thighs)
Olive oil
Red wine vinegar
Honey
Dried herbs (oregano, rosemary)
Garlic
Salt and pepper
Zucchini and yellow squash
Bell peppers
Red onions
Cherry tomatoes

Steps

Step 1: Cut chicken and whisk marinade ingredients.
Step 2: Marinate chicken and soak skewers.
Step 3: Thread chicken and vegetables onto skewers and grill until cooked through.
