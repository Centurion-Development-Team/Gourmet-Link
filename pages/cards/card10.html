Chicken Kabob
Juliakim
Prep time: 1hr 20min | Diffeculty: Hard | 8 Servings | #Lunch

Details

These grilled chicken kabobs are made with marinated chicken and colorful vegetables, all threaded onto skewers and cooked to perfection. An easy dinner that can also be baked in the oven!

Ingredients

Boneless skinless chicken breasts (or thighs)
Olive oil
Red wine vinegar
Honey
Dried herbs (oregano, rosemary)
Garlic
Salt and pepper
Zucchini and yellow squash
Bell peppers
Red onions
Cherry tomatoes

Steps

Step 1: Cut chicken and whisk marinade ingredients.
Step 2: Marinate chicken and soak skewers.
Step 3: Thread chicken and vegetables onto skewers and grill until cooked through.
