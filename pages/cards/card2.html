Honey Garlic Shrimp
Anna Smith
Prep time: 30 min | Diffeculty: Hard | 6 Servings | #Lunch

Details

Are you looking for a super quick, yet flavorful dinner this week? We have it right here for you. This saucy shrimp owes a lot of its flavor to a bold marinade of honey, soy sauce, and garlic, which is music to everyone’s taste buds. For a little extra flavor, we add some minced fresh ginger, but that’s entirely optional.

Ingredients

Honey
Tamari soy sauce (or coconut aminos)
Garlic
Ginger
Crushed red pepper flakes
Shrimp
Oil
Finely diced herbs and sliced green onions (for garnish)

Steps

Step 1: Mix honey, soy sauce, garlic, ginger, and red pepper flakes in a bowl to make the sauce.
Step 2: Toss shrimp with 1/3 of the sauce and marinate for 15-20 minutes.
Step 3: Heat oil in a pan, cook shrimp 1-2 minutes per side until pink. Add remaining sauce, simmer and stir until reduced.
