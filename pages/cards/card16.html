Stir-Fried Tofu
Mario
Prep time: 30 min | Diffeculty: Hard | 8 Servings | #Breakfast

Details

I am sharing one of my very favorite kinds of recipes today! It’s a clean-out-the-fridge, even-if you-can’t- cook-you-can-cook-this, HELP-me-so-hungry, easy, healthy dinner: Tofu Stir Fry! You can use any kind of vegetables you like, the “sauce” is only two ingredients, and the entire shebang is ready in less than 30.

Ingredients

1/4 cup soy sauce
1/4 cup vegetable broth
1 tablespoon honey
16 oz super firm tofu
1 tablespoon soy sauce
2 tablespoons cornstarch
1 tablespoon neutral oil
1 tablespoon sesame oil
1 red bell pepper
1 bunch broccolini
4 garlic cloves
1 tablespoon ginger
1 tablespoon toasted sesame seeds

Steps

Step 1: Mix soy sauce, vegetable broth, and honey for the sauce.
Step 2: Coat tofu with soy sauce and cornstarch, then cook in oil until crispy.
Step 3: Stir-fry vegetables, add garlic and ginger, then combine with tofu and sauce. Top with sesame seeds.
