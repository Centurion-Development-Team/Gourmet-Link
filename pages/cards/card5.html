Carbonara
Cescar
Prep time: 15 min | Diffeculty: Medium | 6 Servings | #Yumm

Details

This is a real spaghetti carbonara recipe made the traditional Italian way, without a single drop of cream. It relies solely on egg and cheese to make a luscious, creamy carbonara sauce. Food fit for a king (or queen!) that proves simple can be magnificent.

Ingredients

400g (14 oz) spaghetti
200g (7 oz) pancetta or bacon, diced
3 large eggs
1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
2 cloves garlic, minced
Salt and black pepper to taste
Fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish)

Steps

Step 1: Cook spaghetti in salted water until al dente. Reserve 1/2 cup pasta water.
Step 2: Sauté diced pancetta or bacon in olive oil until crispy. Add minced garlic and cook for 1 minute.
Step 3: Beat eggs with Parmesan cheese and black pepper. Toss hot pasta with bacon and garlic, then mix in egg mixture off heat. Adjust creaminess with reserved pasta water. Garnish with parsley and serve.
