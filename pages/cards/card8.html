Deviled Eggs
Ali
Prep time: 35 min | Diffeculty: Easy | 2 Servings | #Appertizer

Details

Deviled eggs are easy to make and you can make them even easier by hard boiling your eggs ahead of time. But the key to the BEST deviled eggs is boiling your eggs perfectly and not overboiling and ending up with that green tinge around your yolk. Trust me, no guests want green-tinged deviled eggs.

Ingredients

Hard-boiled eggs
Mayo (or Greek yogurt)
Lemon juice (or vinegar)
Dijon mustard
Garlic powder, salt, and pepper
Toppings: Smoked paprika, fresh chives

Steps

Step 1: Prepare yolk filling by mashing yolks with Greek yogurt, lemon juice, Dijon, garlic powder, salt, and pepper.
Step 2: Fill egg whites with yolk mixture using a spoon, scoop, or piping bag.
Step 3: Sprinkle with toppings and serve fresh.
