Grape Sorbet
Barb Hodgens
#Healthy Desserts #Frozen Treats #Fruit-Based Recipes

Ingredients

1 kg seedless grapes
1 teaspoon lemon zest
1 tablespoon lemon juice

Steps

Step 1: Freeze grapes on a tray for at least 4 hours.
Step 2: Blend frozen grapes with lemon juice and zest until smooth.
Step 3: Scoop and serve immediately or freeze to firm up.
