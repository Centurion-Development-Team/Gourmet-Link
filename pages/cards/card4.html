Cheesy Zucchini Lasagna
Andy
#Cheesy #Low carb,

Ingredients

4 large zucchinis
2 tablespoons olive oil
1 onion, sliced
2 cloves garlic, minced
1 pound ground beef
8 ounces mushrooms, chopped
2 teaspoons garlic powder
1 teaspoon Cajun seasoning
16 ounces tomato sauce
1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

Step 1: Preheat oven to 400°F. Slice zucchini and set aside.
Step 2: Sauté onions and garlic in olive oil, then add ground beef and cook until browned. Stir in mushrooms, tomato sauce, garlic powder, Cajun seasoning, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 5 minutes.
Step 3: Layer zucchini slices, sauce, and cheese in a casserole dish. Repeat layers and top with remaining cheese. Bake covered for 40 minutes, then broil uncovered for 5 minutes. Garnish with parsley and serve.
